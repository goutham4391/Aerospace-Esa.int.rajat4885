ExoMars

Has life ever existed on Mars?

The mission

The ExoMars programme comprises two missions: the first – the Trace Gas Orbiter – launched in 2016 while the second, comprising a rover and surface platform, is awaiting confirmation for launch. Together they will address the question of whether life has ever existed on Mars.

  • 1st European rover
  • 2 m rover drill depth
  • 70m/sol driving on Mars
  • 4 modules
