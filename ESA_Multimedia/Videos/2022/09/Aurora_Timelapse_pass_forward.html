Timelapse video made during ESA astronaut Samantha Cristoforetti’s second mission to the International Space Station, “Minerva”. It shows the Space Station flying into a stunning aurora borealis, a Moon rise and the Moon glinting on the surface of the planet.

You can follow along with the rest of Samantha’s Mission Minerva here at our dedicated Minerva portal, or at Samantha’s personal Twitter and TikTok pages.
