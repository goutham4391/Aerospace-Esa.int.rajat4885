Φ-sat

Artificial intelligence for Earth observation

The mission

Ф-sat-1, an enhancement of the Federated Satellite Systems (FSSCat) mission, is the first experiment to demonstrate how artificial intelligence can be used for Earth observation – in this case, filtering out less than perfect images so that only usable data are returned to Earth. 

The launch

  • Ф-sat-1: 3 September 2020
  • Site: Kourou, French Guiana
  • Rocket: Vega 
  • Instrument: Hyperspectral camera and AI
  • 1st AI for Earth observation
  • FSSCat 2 cubesats
  • 540 km altitude
  • 1 year mission life
