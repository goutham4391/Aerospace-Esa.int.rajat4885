Arctic Weather Satellite

Improving Arctic and global weather forecasts

The mission

The Arctic Weather Satellite mission will provide frequent coverage of Earth for improved nowcasting and numerical weather prediction. 

The instrument

Carrying a cross-track scanning microwave radiometer, the Arctic Weather Satellite mission provides measurements of atmospheric humidity and temperature.

  • 100% global coverage
  • 600 km altitude
  • 120 kg mass
  • 97 mins per orbit
