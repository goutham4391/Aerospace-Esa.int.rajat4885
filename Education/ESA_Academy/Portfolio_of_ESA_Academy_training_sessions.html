ESA / Education / ESA Academy

Since March 2016, the ESA Education Office has been offering University students the opportunity to attend training sessions within the frame of ESA Academy’s Training and Learning Programme. The training sessions are offered in different fields of ESA expertise to complement what they learn at University to better prepare students for a space-related career. Up to 20 training sessions are delivered by ESA and external experts every year and new training sessions are developed in parallel. Below you can find the current portfolio of training sessions, including the last and the next delivery date.
